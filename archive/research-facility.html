Research Facility

A Joplin facility built for emerging battery chemistry and testing.

The MOCAP facility is located on the campus of Missouri Southern State University and was described on the legacy site as a 4,000 square foot lab offering dry-room capability, glove-box access, test equipment, and flexible leasing arrangements.

Facility highlight

600 square foot Munters dry room capable of conditioning to 0.5% relative humidity for lithium-ion material handling.

Facility profile

Tools, access, and support for electrochemical research.

The original site emphasized that the facility gives researchers the tools and equipment needed to develop and test emerging battery chemistry.

The MOCAP research facility is located on the campus of Missouri Southern State University in Joplin, Missouri. The 4,000 square foot facility provides researchers the tools and equipment needed to develop and test emerging battery chemistry.

Exclusive or shared leasing of the facility is available to researchers, corporations, government agencies, universities, and private individuals. Legacy copy states that leasing can be arranged weekly, monthly, or annually.

Technical assistance can be provided by experts in battery research and development, supported by a statewide university network with experience in electrochemistry and specialty materials. Student assistants are also described as available for data gathering and documentation work.

Quick facts

  • 4,000 sq ft facility on the MSSU campus in Joplin
  • 600 sq ft controlled dry room
  • Shared or exclusive leasing options
  • Video conferencing and private office space
  • Campus access with security monitoring

Equipment

Key capabilities listed on the legacy site.

Material handling

  • Munters dry room capable of 0.5% relative humidity
  • VAC Omni-Lab glove box
  • Battery pouch and bag lamination equipment
  • Miniature welders for button and tab work

Testing and measurement

  • Fluke 298 logging multimeters with TrendCapture
  • Arbin Instruments multi-channel cell charge/discharge cycle tester
  • Equipment targeted at chemistry evaluation and product testing

Facility support

  • Video conferencing for distributed collaboration
  • Private office space for team management and staff
  • Small wet bar area for daily refreshments
  • 24/7 access with campus security monitoring
